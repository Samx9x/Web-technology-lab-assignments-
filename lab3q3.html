Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 5 tablespoons soft butter, divided (or to taste)
  • 4 slices bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 4 slices Monterey Jack pepper cheese (or use American cheese)
  • 4 slices tomatoes

Instructions:

Spread about 1 tablespoon of butter into the bottom of a large skillet (or just leave some fat in the pan from the bacon).

Spread 1 tablespoon (can use more) onto one side of each slice of bread.

Place the 4 slices of bread butter-side down onto a large skillet or flat grill.

Top with 1 slice of cheese, 2 slices of tomato, and 2 slices of cooked bacon, then 1 more slice of cheese.

Cover with a slice of bread (butter-side facing outside).

Fry over medium heat, turning once with a spatula, until golden brown on both sides.
